Prof Graeme Black     Contact: graeme.black@manchester.ac.uk

Research Interests: Promoting complementary expertise in detailed diagnostic classification (’deep phenotyping’) aligned to molecular discovery, as a tool to develop novel biological insights that catalyse discovery of interacting networks and inform biological models.

Publications

Prof Kathryn Hentges     Contact: kathryn.hentges@manchester.ac.uk

Research Interests: The process of left-right axis formation as the embryonic heart develops. If this process is disrupted the heart and lungs will not form the correct connections, resulting in abnormal cardiac function and severe birth defects.

Publications

Prof Simon Lovell     Contact: simon.lovell@manchester.ac.uk

Research Interests: Understanding the 3D structure of proteins from three perspectives: sequence-structure relationships, evolution, and protein function.

Publications

Prof Cathy McCrohan     Contact: cathy.mccrohan@manchester.ac.uk

Research Interests: Using the simple ‘nose’ of the fruit fly larva (maggot) to study how a small number of sense cells can identify a large number of different odours. This includes building a computational model which mimics the coding of information that occurs in the nose.
